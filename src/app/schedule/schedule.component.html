Hi, I am tutorial!

Put in course names and hit "GENERATE". That's pretty much it.

1. If a generated schedule doesn't look good enough to you, you could keep hitting the "GENERATE A SCHEDULE" button to find a next fit.

2. A course placed higher in the form has higher priority. In case of an impossible schedule, we may drop the less prioritized courses to give a suboptimal solution.

3. The course names you put in the input boxes must be properly formatted. e.g. CS 225 works while CS225, cs225 and cs 225 don't.

4. Due to the exponential nature of the task at hand, the schedule could not be generated in a reasonable amount of time if you put too many courses in.

Enjoy!
